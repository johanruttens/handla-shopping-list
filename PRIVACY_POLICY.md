# Privacy Policy for Handla

**Last updated:** December 7, 2024

## Overview

Handla is a shopping list app that respects your privacy. We believe your data belongs to you and should stay on your device.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Handla is designed to work entirely offline. All your data, including:

- Your name (used only for personalized greetings)
- Shopping lists
- Favorite items
- App preferences

...is stored locally on your device using SQLite database and is never transmitted to any server.

## No Analytics or Tracking

- We do not use any analytics services
- We do not track your usage
- We do not collect crash reports
- We do not use any third-party SDKs that collect data

## No Account Required

Handla does not require you to create an account or sign in. There is no user registration, and therefore no personal information is collected or stored on external servers.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all your data will be permanently removed from your device.

## Third-Party Services

Handla does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data.

## Children's Privacy

Handla does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any personal data from any users, this app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
