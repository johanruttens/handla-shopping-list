Handla - Your Smart Shopping List Companion

Tired of forgetting items at the grocery store? Handla is the beautifully designed shopping list app that makes grocery shopping effortless and even fun!

KEY FEATURES:

• Simple & Intuitive
Create and manage your shopping list in seconds. Add items with just a tap, mark them as bought with a satisfying animation, and stay organized effortlessly.

• Works Offline
No internet? No problem! Handla stores everything locally on your device, so your list is always available - even in the basement of that big supermarket.

• Smart Organization
Sort items by name, category, or date. Filter between items to buy and already purchased. Search through your list instantly.

• Favorites & Quick Add
Save your frequently bought items as favorites. Quickly add recent items or suggestions to your list with one tap.

• Beautiful Design
Inspired by Apple and Bang & Olufsen, Handla features a minimalist, elegant interface with smooth animations and haptic feedback that makes every interaction delightful.

• Dark Mode
Easy on the eyes during those late-night shopping runs. Automatic system theme support included.

• Multi-Language Support
Available in English, Dutch, German, Spanish, Swedish, and French.

• Celebration Mode
Complete your entire list and enjoy a fun confetti celebration! Shopping has never felt so rewarding.

• Swipe Actions
Quickly edit or delete items with intuitive swipe gestures.

• Undo Support
Accidentally deleted something? No worries - just tap undo to bring it back.

Handla is perfect for individuals, couples, and families who want a no-fuss, beautiful shopping list that just works. No account required, no subscriptions, no ads - just pure shopping list bliss.
